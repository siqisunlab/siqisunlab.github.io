About

I am a Postdoctoral Associate at Fudan University, where I lead the Causal Learning Research Group. My research lies at the intersection of causal machine learning, medical image analysis, and clinical AI. I develop principled methods that move beyond correlation-based learning — building AI systems that reason causally and generalize reliably in real clinical settings.

My applied work focuses on AI-enabled diagnostics for retinal diseases and neonatal health, in close collaboration with clinicians and medical researchers. I am broadly interested in designing AI systems that are not only accurate, but interpretable and trustworthy for high-stakes medical decision-making.
